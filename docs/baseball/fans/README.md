# PSIS — Baseball for the Lifelong Fan

**A conversation about pitching — not a software manual.**

---

## Who This Is For

You have watched baseball for decades. You remember when the box score was the bible. You trust what you see with your own eyes. You may roll your eyes at "analytics" — and you are still welcome here.

This folder is **about baseball**. Not apps. Not replacing tradition. Not telling you that everything you learned at the ballpark was wrong.

---

## What This Is

PSIS — Pitch Sequence Intelligence System — started as a way to remember what happened **at-bat by at-bat** during a pitching outing. The ideas behind it are older than any computer: pitch sequencing, competing with runners on, knowing when you won or lost a battle with a hitter.

The software is just a scorebook that never forgets. **These pages are the thinking behind the scorebook.**
