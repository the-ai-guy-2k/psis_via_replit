# Using PSIS for Self-Development

PSIS is a mirror. **You** still do the work.

This page is about habits — with or without the app on your phone.

---

## Goal Setting

### Bad goals

- "Zero ERA"
- "More strikeouts than last guy"
- "Never give up a homer"

### Good goals (EABR-aligned)

| Goal | Why |
|------|-----|
| Positive session delta in 3 of next 4 outings | Wins at-bats, not luck |
| Cut walks in half over a month | Fewer bad units |
| Strand runners in 2 of 3 jam innings | Compete with traffic |
| First-pitch strike 60%+ | Process you control |

Write goals down. Review with your coach every two weeks.

---

## Bullpen Review

### Before the pen

- Know **one** focus from last outing's review
- Treat live ABs or sim situations like game at-bats — win each one mentally

### After the pen

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Did I execute the fix we identified? | Connect pen to game |
| Which sequences felt right? | Build your arsenal map |
| Where did I fall into old habits? | Catch drift early |

Your coach may log pen at-bats the same way as games. **Same rules, same honesty.**

---

## Game Review

### Night of (5 minutes)

- One sentence: what went well
- One sentence: what hurt
- Sleep

### Next day (with coach or notes)

1. Session delta
2. Inning deltas
3. One pattern
4. One adjustment

See [Learning_From_Every_Outing.md](./Learning_From_Every_Outing.md) for the full method.

## Tracking Improvement

| You track… | Over… | Tool |
|------------|-------|------|
| Session delta | Each outing | Coach's PSIS summary or your notebook |
| Walk bad units | Monthly | Trend line |
| LOB good units | Monthly | "Do I compete in jams?" |
| Your 1–5 consistency self-rates | Weekly | Journal |

**Trend beats snapshot.** A chart of four outings tells more than one number.

---

## Working With Coaches

| You bring | Coach brings |
|-----------|--------------|
| Honesty about how you felt | EABR log and film |
| Willingness to own mistakes | Specific drills |
| Questions | Answers without shame |
| Effort in bullpen | Plan for next opponent |

**Ask:** "What did my delta say, and does video agree?"

Disagreement is okay. **Silence is not.**

---

## Preparing for the Next Outing

### Two days before

- Know opponent tendencies (if available)
- Know your plan categories: attack, put-away, get-back-in-count

### Day before

- Visualize **one at-bat at a time** — not the whole game
- Rest

### Day of

- Trust preparation
- Compete for good units — strikeouts, outs, stranded runners all count
- Reset after bad at-bats — the next hitter does not know your last pitch

## Self-Development Without the App

| Habit | Frequency |
|-------|-----------|
| At-bat reset on the mound | Every hitter |
| Post-outing two-sentence journal | Every game |
| One bullpen focus | Every pen |
| Monthly trend talk with coach | Every 2–4 weeks |

PSIS makes the log easier. **The habits make you better.**
